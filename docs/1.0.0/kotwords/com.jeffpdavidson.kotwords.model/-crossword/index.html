A representation of a crossword puzzle.

Parameters

title

The title of the puzzle.

author

The author of the puzzle.

copyright

The copyright of the puzzle.

notes

Optional notes about the puzzle.

grid

The grid of Squares in the form of a list of rows going from top to bottom, with each row going from left to right.

acrossClues

Mapping from across clue number to the clue for that number.

downClues

Mapping from down clue number to the clue for that number.

hasHtmlClues

Whether clue contents are in HTML.

acrossWords

Optional specification of all words in the Across direction. Only needed for irregular numbering/ cluing schemes.

downWords

Optional specification of all words in the Down direction. Only needed for irregular numbering/ cluing schemes.
